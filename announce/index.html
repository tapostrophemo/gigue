Gigue is will be a lightweight Java web development framework. It was will be ported from CodeIgniter, a lightweight PHP web development framework, and therefore shares CodeIgniter's architecture and approach.

Gigue is will be freely available and with an open source license. (Most likely it will be released under the Artistic License 2.0.)

Gigue is currently under development; alpha releases may become available during the Fall/Winter of 2010.
